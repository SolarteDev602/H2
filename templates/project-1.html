The Challenge

Procacci Brothers wanted to raise awareness for their non-profit program, The “Estrella Naciente” Scholarship fund, which provides educational opportunities for young leaders in the Hispanic community. The challenge was to create a video that could showcase the struggles of these students and highlight the company’s efforts to reward their hard work.

Strategy

We developed a documentary-style video that would take the viewers on a journey through the struggles of these students and the generosity of the Procacci organization. We captured interviews with scholarship recipients, highlighting their stories and aspirations. We also spent time touring and filming the Procacci facilities to showcase the company's dedication to providing healthy, high-quality produce.

Results

The resulting video was a touching tribute to the struggles and triumphs of the scholarship recipients, and the generous contributions of the Procacci Brothers. It helped raise awareness for the scholarship program and showcased the company's commitment to the Hispanic community. The video was widely shared on social media and received positive feedback from viewers who were inspired by the student's determination to succeed despite their obstacles. Overall, the video succeeded in achieving its goal of spreading awareness for the scholarship fund and highlighting the charitable efforts of the Procacci Brothers.
